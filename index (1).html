I am a PhD student in the Department of Pharmacy – Drug Sciences at the University of Bari Aldo Moro. I hold a Master's degree in Chemistry and am a registered member of the Italian Order of Chemists. During my PhD I spent six months as a visiting researcher at Åbo Akademi University in Turku, Finland, where I had previously carried out a three-month research stay during my bachelor's.

My research focuses on developing high-performance biosensors based on organic field-effect transistors (EGOFET) and surface plasmon resonance (SPR).

I also hold a second-level Master (MBA) in Economics and Management of Healthcare Organizations, with a thesis on data handling, privacy and blockchain carried out during an internship at ARESS (the Apulia regional health agency), in the Digital Transition and Privacy Office. I have presented my work at several national and international conferences.
